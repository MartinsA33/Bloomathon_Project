What is the purpose of Kinder?

Kinder is on stop shop on learning how to be kind and being consistent with it.
Kinder helps gives kindness tasks each day for you to complete. When you complete
a task it counts towards a streak, each day you complete a Kinder task you build and
maintain that streak, when you don't do a Kinder task the streak is broken. Basically
it is duolingo for kindness. But, instead of learning languages you are learning how
to be kinder.
